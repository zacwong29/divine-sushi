About Us


This establishment dates back to 1996, with our expertise still growing.

Here at Divine Sushi, we utilise ancient techniques as well as our newly learnt skills to provide the most unique eating experience possible for our customers.

We have our own Japanese garden behind the restaurant that is regularly maintained and ensures we have plenty of fresh ingredients for our dishes.

All of our kitchen supplies are bought from Japan and some were used by our ancestors a long time ago.
